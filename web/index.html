NYC moves
1M+ trips
per day.

From 2015 to 2024, New York City's taxis — Yellow, Green, and For-Hire Vehicles — carried over 1 billion passengers. Every dot of this chart is a month's worth of journeys across the city.

Every week
has a rhythm.

Rush hours, late-night Friday surges, quiet Sunday mornings — the city's movement follows predictable patterns. Yellow taxis peak at midday; demand shifts toward FHV after midnight.

2019 baseline week highlighted

Then
March 2020
happened.

When New York issued the PAUSE order on March 22, 2020, trip volume collapsed overnight. Yellow taxi ridership fell ~97% in April 2020 compared to the year before.
